About Us

PuneHelps is a community-driven platform created to bring people together in the spirit of mutual aid and support. Our mission is to connect individuals in Pune who are looking to offer or receive help, whether it’s through volunteering, job opportunities, or sharing resources. In today’s fast-paced world, neighbors often don’t realize the incredible support network that exists just around the corner. PuneHelps bridges this gap by offering a space where people can post requests for help, browse volunteer opportunities, or offer their skills and resources to others in need. From assisting the elderly with daily tasks to finding trusted local job listings, PuneHelps empowers individuals to make a difference in their community. Every small act of kindness contributes to building a stronger, more resilient Pune. Join us today and be a part of this growing movement of compassion and collaboration. Together, we can uplift one another and create a community where everyone feels supported.
